Finding Harmony

Asian cultures influence me throughout my entire life. I admire Minor White, an early editor of Aperature magazine, for dedicating the last years of his life to eastern philosophies. Part of the success of Minor White is that he, too, observed other photographers. He appreciated Alfred Stieglitz' focus on the sequence of photographs, Edward Weston's use of tones and textures of nature, and Ansel Adam's framing of a scene.

While Minor White looked to Zen Buddhism, I look to Daoism which places emphasis over humanity. The main components of Daoist belief are yin and yang, which are in constant balance with each other to keep at equilibrium.
