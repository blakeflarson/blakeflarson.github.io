Learning Typography

Collection of my typographic studies from graphic design 1. Click to view.

The assignment was to write a paper about a topic of a social issue. I wrote about "Nomophobia" which is essentially "smartphone addiction". As handy as smartphones are, I find that they become a distraction. People get too caught up in their phones without appreciating what's around them.

Magazine Spreads

The next step was to create magazine spreads using the text from my paper. The focus was to create 3 spreads showing: a dominant image with subordinate text, dominant text with subordinate image, and a spread of our choosing.

Logo Design

The assignment was to design a logo for a "make-believe" business that is related to our topic. I chose to create a logo for a smartphone application that keeps track of phone usage.
